# A Little Bookshelf

A personal bookshelf with 1,000 real books pulled from the [Open Library](https://openlibrary.org) collection.

Browse by genre, search by title or author, sort by rating or year — and keep track of what you want to read, what you're reading, and what you've finished.

**[View the bookshelf →](https://diprekshya.github.io/LittleBookshelf)**

---

Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no backend. Reading lists are saved to your browser's local storage.
